  5. College Dropout (2004)

    Last, but not least is The College Dropout, Kanye's first studio album. This album features

    artists such as Jay-Z, Ludacris, Common, and Jamie Foxx. Previously known only for his production,

    this album introduces us to Kanye, the artist. It also features his own production and brings a

    different look and sound to rap culture. Kanye came onto the scene unique and unapologetic, beginning

    a successful musical career.
